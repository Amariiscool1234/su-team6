## Description 
> League Finder is a local recreational sports web app that helps players find teams, games, and substitute opportunities in their area. The app connects players looking to join rec sports with team captains and leagues that need players or subs.The motivation for League Finder is to make it easier for people to stay active, meet others, and participate in local sports without having to already know a team. The goal of the app is to solve the problem of players struggling to find teams and captains struggling to fill roster spots. League Finder provides services such as free-agent boards, skill-level matching, roster management, game reminders, and player feedback.
## App Functions
1. Customer (the user with the customer role):
   1. Create/modify customer profile - Customers can create an account and add personal information such as name,    location, preferred sports, availability, and skill level. They can update this information anytime from their profile page.
   2. View available services - Customers can browse available teams, leagues, open roster spots, and sub opportunities based on sport, location, date, and skill level.
   3. Subscribe to available services - Customers can request to join a team, sign up as a free agent, or offer to sub for a game. Once accepted, they can receive updates and reminders for upcoming games.
   4. Write reviews for subscribed services - Customers can leave feedback about teams, leagues, and game experiences after participating in a game or joining a team.
